This article outlines the principles and laboratory methods underlying quantum randomness, with specific reference to the publicly accessible quantum random number stream produced by the Australian National University (ANU). 1. Introduction
PRNGs offer statistically robust sequences but remain deterministic under known internal states. For cryptography and other high-stakes processes, determinism poses inherent risks. QRNGs address these concerns by leveraging fundamentally unpredictable quantum events.
